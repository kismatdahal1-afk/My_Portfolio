From Science to Software

I completed my SEE at Shree Makpa Secondary School (3.60 GPA) before pursuing Plus Two in Science (Physical Group with Computer Science) at Reliance International Academy. Now I'm pursuing a BSc CSIT in Kathmandu — bridging analytical thinking with modern development.

As a self-taught web developer, I've built my stack from the ground up — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python — and applied them to real-world projects including e-commerce platforms (Gaunle-Mart, Rasilo-Suntala), banking and school management systems, a note management app, and a BMW sales analysis processing over 15,000+ data points.

I'm passionate about UI/UX design, full-stack development, and data analysis. Every project is an opportunity to craft something that works beautifully and performs flawlessly.
